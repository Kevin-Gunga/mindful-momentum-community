about

The mindful momentum community is an organisation founded on the identified need of equal access to mental health services to all members of the community regardless of background, belief,status or ethnicity.

we hope to bridge the gap between the professionals in mental health i.e psychologists, psychiatrists and the members of the society.We also aim to create mental health awareness which will turn eradicate the stigma that has plagued our society about mental illness and related issues.

    goals

  • the major goal of this initiative is to eradicate stigma and raise awareness on the subject of mental health by giving education in form of psychological counselling and mental health talks.
  • the other goal of this foundation is to provide psychosocial support in order to try and address the mental health issues identified while working collaboratively with the company management and employees as well.

    objectives

  • to create educative and informative avenues for the delivery of accurate and professional information on mental health and issues surrouning it.
  • to make psychological/mental health services available and accessible to all in order to promote mental, emotional and spiritual wellness.

    mission

  • to ensure equitable access of all community members to research proven information on mental health and also quality services in order to promote their holistic wellness.This in turn go a long way in aiding in the eradicationof the stigma that plagues the subject of mental health in the country due to limited access to information about mental health.
  • to be an organisation that promotes and supports the well-being of all community members so as to achieve the nation building agenda.To serve and transform.
